== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Q. Can users receive more than one discount simultaneously? =

Ans: Absolutely! You can apply multiple discounts to a single order. This flexibility allows you to craft comprehensive discount strategies such as combining bulk discounts, tiered discounts, and BOGO offers as defined in your rule sets.

= Q. Can I control when a discount starts and ends? =

Ans: Yes, you have full control over the timing of your discounts. For each discount rule, you can specify both a start date and an end date, ensuring your promotions run exactly when you intend.

= Q. What types of discounts can I create with this plugin? =

Ans: With this powerful plugin, you have a variety of discount options at your disposal, including:

<ul>
<li><strong>Bulk Discounts:</strong> Apply discounts storewide or to specific categories, products, or attributes based on the quantity purchased.</li>
<li><strong>Tiered Discounts:</strong> Offer escalating discounts when customers purchase in bulk. For example, buying more units entitles them to greater discounts.</li>
<li><strong>BOGO Offers:</strong> Set up a variety of "Buy One, Get One" scenarios such as "Buy X Get X," "Buy X Get Y," or conditional offers like "Buy Product A and get 50% off Product B."</li>
</ul>

= Q. Can the plugin offer discounts based on the customer's location? =

Ans: Yes, the plugin enables you to set discounts based on the customer‚Äôs shipping location, perfect for tailoring your discounts to specific regions or markets.

= Q. Does the plugin support discounts based on the user's role? =

Ans: Indeed, you can configure discounts to apply only to certain user roles, providing an excellent way to offer exclusive perks to members, subscribers, or specific customer groups.

= Q. Can discounts be applied based on the payment method chosen? =

Ans: Yes, the plugin allows you to encourage the use of preferred payment methods by offering discounts when customers choose a specific gateway for their transaction.
